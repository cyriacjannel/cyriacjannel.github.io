Docteur T. Dance

TweenMax animation

View the project

Doctor T. Dance is a short animation coded in JavaScript and based on TweenMax library.

The animation depicts Doctor T, a protagonist of the strategy game "Boom Beach", dancing frantically on the tube of the early 2000s; "Blue" by Eiffel 65.
