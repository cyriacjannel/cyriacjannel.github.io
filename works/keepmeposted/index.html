Keepmeposted

Vacancy website

Visit the website

Keepmeposted is a vacancy website which brings job seekers and employers together. It allows job seekers to apply for a job directly online.

I worked on Keepmeposted during an internship at Anchovy, a digital marketing agency based in Malta. My mission was to search the various issues on the platform and find a way to fix them. I analyzed the website with users in various situations to understand what can be a barrier for them. This allowed me to improve the user experience by simplifying the architecture and the navigation of the website. The main point was to highlight the two main actions of the website, finding a job or posting a job. I also worked on the interfaces of the website and create a template for emails to make the product more consistent and trendy.
