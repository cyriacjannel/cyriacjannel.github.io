Ressors Ta Table

Mobile app

RTT is an mobile app wich allow users to meet around table tennis event.

The mobile app integrates a gamification system, taking inspiration from capture the flag game. The user can sign up for tournaments on tables close to him and try to win territories by conquering these tables. By encouraging users to expand their territory, the app encourages them to repeat the experience. Therefore, RTT creates a real community of table tennis players in a city.
