Kula

Mobile app

Kula is a mobile app for publishing ephemeral content that can only be consulted around the place where it is published.

The app allows users to share multimedia content; text, photo, video or audio. This content may be viewed by other users only around the place where it was originally published. Users can rate the content they consult, to increase their visibility on the map.
