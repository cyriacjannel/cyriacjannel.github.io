Robotime

Useless robot

View the project

Robotime is a small robot fully coded in HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

This charming android evolving during the day. Like most humans, he wakes up at sunrise and falls asleep at nightfall. It has a multitude of lights and gauges lighting up and filling up throughout the day.
