Owlook

Mobile app

Owlook is a mobile app which allow the users to discover a city from another point of view and in a playful way.

The application provide users wishing to visit a city to search small owls. Once the user finds a bird, he can catch it and enjoy an aerial panoramic to discover the place where he is. Go on an aventure to discover new cities to catch them all !
