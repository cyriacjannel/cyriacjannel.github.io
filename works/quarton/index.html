Quarton

Time bank service

Quarton is a time bank service aroud the rearrangement and the home improvement.

The web platform allows all the users to offer or ask for assistance, in rearranging, lending tools and assembling a piece of furniture. Everyone can help other people and being helped in return, by bartering their time without exchanging money.
