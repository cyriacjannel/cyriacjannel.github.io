Feelings

Series of illustration

Feelings is a series of illustrations depicting the characters from the cartoon SpongeBob SquarePants.

These characters were realized on Adobe Illustrator, as part of a graphic design project, in order to familiarize myself with the different tools and the interface of the software. Each poster had to deal with the same theme with a similar graphic style.
